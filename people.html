Principal Investigator

Bo Zhang 张博

Associate Professor · Department of Biomedical Engineering · SUSTech

I am Bo Zhang, a Principal Investigator in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). I received my Bachelor's degree from the College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering, Peking University in 2010, under the supervision of Professor Jin Zhang, Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Chemistry, Stanford University in 2016, under the guidance of Professor Hongjie Dai, Academician of the US National Academy of Sciences and National Academy of Medicine. I joined the Department of Biomedical Engineering at SUSTech in 2021. I have published over 50 high-level research papers, with senior author publications in Nature Biomedical Engineering, Nature Computational Science, Angewandte Chemie, Journal of Hepatology, Water Research, Nano Letters, and Analytical Chemistry, with over 11,000 total citations (Google Scholar) and an H-index of 32.

Faculty

Pan-Lin Shao 邵攀霖

Professor · Guangzhou Medical University

Pan-Lin Shao received his Ph.D. from the Institute of Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2011 (supervisor: Prof. Song Ye), and conducted postdoctoral research at the National University of Singapore from 2012 to 2014 (supervisor: Prof. Yu Zhao). He joined SUSTech as a Research Associate Professor and doctoral supervisor in 2018. His research focuses on the development of novel in vitro diagnostic technologies and asymmetric synthetic methodologies. He has published nearly 40 high-level papers, including over 10 first- or corresponding-author papers in J. Am. Chem. Soc., Angew. Chem. Int. Ed., and ACS Catalysis, and has been granted more than ten national invention patents.

Ying Liu 刘莹

Research Assistant Professor

Ying Liu received her Ph.D. in Chemistry from Peking University in 2016. She worked in equity investment in the health industry from 2016 to 2019, then conducted postdoctoral research at the Department of Biomedical Engineering, SUSTech from 2019 to 2021. Her research focuses on nucleic acid and protein detection, liquid biopsy, and clinical applications. She has published nearly 20 SCI papers as first or corresponding author in Nature Biomedical Engineering, Analytical Chemistry, and Chemical Science, and has led multiple national and provincial grants. The viral nucleic acid automated extraction technology she developed has received US FDA Emergency Use Authorization.

Senior Research Scholar & Postdoc

Hongjun Xiao 肖宏军

Senior Research Scholar

Hongjun Xiao holds a Ph.D. in Science. His research applies interdisciplinary approaches across biology, chemistry, and materials science to study liver diseases—including liver fibrosis and hepatocellular carcinoma—at the molecular, therapeutic, and detection levels. Specific directions include: (1) molecular mechanisms of extracellular matrix proteins in liver disease; (2) hepatic stellate cell-based therapeutic strategies; (3) high-throughput detection methods for liver disease-associated pathogens HBV, HCV, and HIV. He also works on the development and application of novel biotechnologies.

📧 xiaohj [at] sustech.edu.cn

Yanping Sun 孙燕平

Postdoctoral Researcher

Yanping Sun holds a Ph.D. in Science and is originally from Yantai, Shandong. He completed his undergraduate and doctoral training at the University of Science and Technology Beijing. His research integrates organic chemistry, analytical chemistry, coordination chemistry, and surface chemistry to achieve early in vitro detection of disease biomarkers at the molecular, microscopic interface, and macroscopic signal levels. Specific directions include: (1) site-specific nucleobase modification and next-generation sequencing for early in vitro diagnosis; (2) metal-enhanced luminescence-based AIE-type NIR gold nanocluster composites for ultrasensitive detection of disease biomarkers.
