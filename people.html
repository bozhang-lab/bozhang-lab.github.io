Bo Zhang 张博

Associate Professor · Department of Biomedical Engineering · SUSTech

I am Bo Zhang, a Principal Investigator in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). I received my Bachelor's degree from the College of Chemistry and Molecular Engineering, Peking University in 2010, under the supervision of Professor Jin Zhang, Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Chemistry, Stanford University in 2016, under the guidance of Professor Hongjie Dai, Academician of the US National Academy of Sciences and National Academy of Medicine.

I joined the Department of Biomedical Engineering at SUSTech in 2021. I have published over 50 high-level research papers, with senior author publications in Nature Biomedical Engineering, Nature Computational Science, Angewandte Chemie, Journal of Hepatology, Water Research, Nano Letters, and Analytical Chemistry, with over 11,000 total citations (Google Scholar) and an H-index of 32.
